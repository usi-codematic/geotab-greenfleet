# GreenFleet Commander - Demo Video Script (3 Minutes)

## Setup Before Recording
- Log into MyGeotab with demo database
- Install GreenFleet Commander Add-In (paste config.json)
- Ensure data has loaded (wait for dashboard to populate)
- Open in Chrome, full-screen mode
- Test each tab quickly to confirm data loads

## Script

### Opening (0:00 - 0:20)
**[Screen: GreenFleet Commander Dashboard loaded]**

> "Fleet electrification is no longer a question of if, but when. But how do you know which vehicles to convert first? Meet GreenFleet Commander — an AI-powered sustainability intelligence platform built directly into MyGeotab."

### Act 1: The Dashboard (0:20 - 0:45)
**[Screen: Point to summary cards, hover over emissions trend chart]**

> "Our dashboard instantly calculates your fleet's carbon footprint. This fleet of [X] vehicles has generated [X] tonnes of CO2 this month. The emissions trend shows daily patterns, and we can see which vehicles consume the most fuel."

**[Screen: Scroll to EV Readiness table]**

> "But the real insight is here — every vehicle gets an EV Readiness Score from 0 to 100."

### Act 2: EV Scoring (0:45 - 1:15)
**[Screen: Click on a top-scored vehicle to expand breakdown]**

> "The score analyzes five factors: daily distance patterns, trip predictability, idle time — which is wasted fuel an EV eliminates — charging opportunity based on return-to-base frequency, and trip compatibility. This vehicle scores [X] because it drives under 100 km daily and returns to base every night — perfect for overnight charging."

### Act 3: The Simulator (1:15 - 1:55)
**[Screen: Click Simulator tab, drag slider from 0 to ~15 vehicles]**

> "Here's where it gets exciting. The electrification simulator lets you model the impact. Watch as I convert the top 15 vehicles..."

**[Screen: Point to metrics updating in real-time]**

> "[X] tonnes of CO2 eliminated over five years. Over $[X] in fuel savings. The system automatically prioritizes the highest-scoring vehicles to maximize your return."

**[Screen: Scroll to show recommended order table]**

> "And here's the exact order — with annual savings per vehicle."

### Act 4: AI Assistant (1:55 - 2:25)
**[Screen: Click AI Assistant tab, click "EV Recommendations" quick button]**

> "Need deeper insights? Ask Geotab Ace in natural language."

**[Screen: Show loading animation, then Ace response appearing]**

> "Ace analyzes your actual fleet data and returns structured recommendations with reasoning — no SQL, no manual reports."

### Act 5: Map, Leaderboard, Report (2:25 - 2:50)
**[Screen: Quick switch to Map tab — show color-coded markers]**

> "The fleet map shows every vehicle color-coded by EV readiness."

**[Screen: Quick switch to Leaderboard tab]**

> "And the eco-driving leaderboard gamifies sustainable behavior — drivers earn badges for efficient driving."

**[Screen: Switch to Reports tab, click Download PDF]**

> "One click generates a complete sustainability report for stakeholders."

### Closing (2:50 - 3:00)
**[Screen: Return to Dashboard]**

> "GreenFleet Commander: your fleet's roadmap to zero emissions. Built with Geotab API, Geotab Ace, and Claude AI — entirely through vibe coding."

## Key Talking Points (if needed)
- Built as a MyGeotab Add-In — zero installation, works inside the platform
- EV scoring algorithm uses real trip data, not assumptions
- Simulator provides concrete financial justification for fleet managers
- Ace integration brings natural language AI directly into fleet decisions
- n8n automation provides 24/7 monitoring without manual intervention
- All data processing happens client-side for speed and privacy
